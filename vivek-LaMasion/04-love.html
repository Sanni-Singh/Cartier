DESIGN

Love is the expression of Cartier’s design vision. It presents the perfection of clean lines and precise proportions: an oval bracelet formed of two rigid arcs that must be screwed together.

THE LEGEND AND THE HISTORY

Created in New York in the 1970s, the Love bracelet is an icon of jewellery design. A close fitting, oval bracelet, worn on the wrist and removed with a gold screwdriver. Passionate and unconditional, it seals intense romances.
